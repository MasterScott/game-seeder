  • Based on The.Surge.The.Good.the.Bad.and.the.Augmented-CODEX ISO release: codex-the.surge.the.good.the.bad.and.the.augmented.iso (22,984,916,992 bytes)
  • Game version: 42854 (SVN); 5 DLCs included and activated: CREO Special Employee Kit, Fire & Ice Weapon Pack, A Walk in the Park, Cutting Edge Pack, The Good the Bad and the Augmented
  • 100% Lossless & MD5 Perfect: all files are identical to originals after installation
  • NOTHING ripped, NOTHING re-encoded
  • Selective download feature: you may skip downloading and installing of speech files you don’t need and movies in 720p resolution
  • Significantly smaller archive size (compressed from 21.4 to 3.9~4.5 GB, depending on selected components)
  • Installation takes 7-20 minutes (depending on your system)
  • After-install integrity check so you could make sure that everything installed properly
  • HDD space after installation: 23 GB
  • To change the language, edit “language.ini” in game root and set game_language_text string to: english, french, italian, german, spanish, polish, russian, chinese, japanese or brazilian_portuguese. game_language_voice string may be only english, french and german
  • At least 2 GB of free RAM (inc. virtual) required for installing this repack
Game Description
A catastrophic event has knocked you out during the first day on the job… you wake up equipped with a heavy-grade exoskeleton, in a destroyed section of the complex. Robots gone haywire, insane augmented co-workers and rogue AI – everything wants you dead.

Defy deadly enemies and huge bosses in tight, visceral melee combat. Target and slice specific limbs off your foes, with a next-gen loot system where you loot what you dismember. Equip, upgrade and craft new weapons and armors sliced from enemies, and make yourself stronger through a fresh take on leveling-up.
