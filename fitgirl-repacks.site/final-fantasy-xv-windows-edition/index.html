Selective Download

You can skip downloading of selective and optional files you don’t need. Here is the list of such files:

  • fg-selective-english.bin (recommended for all languages w/o their own voiceovers)
  • fg-selective-french.bin
  • fg-selective-german.bin
  • fg-selective-japanese.bin
  • fg-selective-russian.bin
  • fg-optional-credits.bin (long boring credits background)
  • fg-optional-japanese-videos.bin (you need those ONLY if you want to play in Japanese)
  • fg-optional-multiplayer.bin (files you may need if you want to try multiplayer)

In example, if you want to launch the game with German UI/Subtitles/Voiceovers – skip all “selective/optional” files, but download fg-selective-german.bin & all main files (01-15).

Remember, that at least one speech pack must be downloaded and installed.
