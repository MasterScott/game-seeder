Lossy VS Lossless

I don’t like lossy compression, but just like NieR Automata, this game proves, that sometimes developers have to use brains.

Videos in MVSCI are present in FullHD with extremely hight bitrate, up to 40 MBbit/second with 30 FPS.

That’s why I made an option for you to download much smaller archive with re-encoded videos.

They still look very good at bitrate of 8~9 MBit/sec at 1920×1080 resolution. You can download this sample and compare:

Mirror 1: Zippyshare
Mirror 2: Multiupload

If you don’t like the quality of re-encoded files, get original ones. Remember – the choice is always yours, not mine 😉
